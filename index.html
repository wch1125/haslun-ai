The Approach

Legal practice generates enormous structured information—checklists, conditions, defined terms, cross-references, deadlines. Yet we manage this complexity with Word documents and email chains.

These projects treat legal workflows as structured data: version-controlled, queryable, with clear audit trails. The goal isn't to replace legal judgment—it's to reduce the cognitive load of complex transactions so lawyers can focus on what matters.
