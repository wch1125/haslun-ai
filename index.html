Experiments in using AI to build better infrastructure for legal work—document tracking, defined terms, closing checklists—the plumbing that makes transactions run.

The idea

Legal practice generates enormous structured information—checklists, conditions, cross-references, deadlines. We manage most of it in Word documents and email chains.

These projects treat legal workflows as structured data, surfacing insights and reducing the cognitive load of complex transactions.
